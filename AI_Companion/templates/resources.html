Breathing Techniques

Calm your nervous system with guided breathing exercises

4-7-8 Breathing

The relaxing breath: Inhale 4s, hold 7s, exhale 8s

2-5 minutes Reduces anxiety

Box Breathing

Equal parts: Inhale 4s, hold 4s, exhale 4s, hold 4s

3-10 minutes Improves focus

Coherent Breathing

Slow rhythm: Inhale 5s, exhale 5s for heart coherence

5-20 minutes Balances nervous system

Why Breathing Works

Reduces Stress

Activates parasympathetic nervous system to calm the body

Improves Focus

Increases oxygen flow to the brain for better concentration

Enhances Recovery

Helps body recover from stress responses more quickly
