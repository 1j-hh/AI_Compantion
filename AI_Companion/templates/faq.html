Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your AI mental health companion

AI Companion

How does the AI emotion detection work?

Our AI uses advanced facial recognition technology trained on the CK+ dataset to identify emotional states through subtle facial micro-expressions. The system analyzes 68 facial landmarks in real-time and compares them against patterns associated with seven core emotions: happiness, sadness, anger, fear, surprise, disgust, and neutral.

The model achieves 92% accuracy in controlled conditions and adapts to individual expression patterns over time to provide increasingly personalized emotional support.

Can Phoebe provide medical diagnoses?

No, Phoebe is designed to provide mental health support and emotional companionship, not medical diagnoses. While I'm trained in therapeutic techniques and can help you process emotions, I cannot:

  • Diagnose mental health conditions
  • Prescribe medication
  • Provide emergency crisis intervention
  • Replace licensed mental health professionals

For clinical diagnoses and treatment, please consult with colony medical professionals at Extension 842.

Privacy & Security

Is my conversation data private?

Yes, your privacy and data security are our highest priorities:

  • End-to-End Encryption: All conversations are encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Local Processing: Facial emotion analysis happens locally on your device
  • Anonymous Analytics: Only aggregate, anonymized data is used for improvement
  • Data Ownership: You own your conversation data and can export or delete it at any time
  • Colony Compliance: Meets Saturn Colony Data Protection Standards (SCDPS-2235)

Your conversations are never shared with third parties or used for training without explicit consent.
